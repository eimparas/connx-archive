OVERVIEW
The ADSL wireless Router combines 802.11g Wireless Access Point , ADSL2+ modem, router, four-ports switch, it offers quick access to the wired and wireless network.
The device supports downstream rate up to 12/24 Mbps with capability of ADSL2/ADSL2+, and upstream rate up to 1Mbps.
